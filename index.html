You take it, and read 'what is the colour of my hair?'
Looking at the 'lockpad', you see that rather than having numbers on it, it has the whole alphabet.
What is the colour of your kidnapper's hair?

You put the letters into the lockpad- you hear a resounding click
as the locks open.
Slowly turning the handle, you find you can open the door now.
